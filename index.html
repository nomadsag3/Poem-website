When the Stars Remember

When the stars remember my name, Let them whisper it to the wind— Not as a song of sorrow, But as a tale the cosmos pinned. In silent scripts of silver light, Across the endless sky, Let echoes of my dreaming soul In constellations lie. For I have walked where no feet tread, Through shadows soft and wide, And in the hush of midnight flame, My legacy shall ride.
— Written by Nomadsage
